behind the spices and recipies available around the fishermen community.

its tranquil yet explosive taste.




OUR ATTRACTIONS




HOUSE BOAT DINING

Embark on a serene house boating experience through Amritapuri's breathtaking backwaters,
where the tranquil waters meet the lush greenery of Kerala's countryside.
As you glide along the winding waterways, indulge in the splendor of traditional Kerala cuisine, carefully crafted to delight your senses.
Savor the bold flavors of spicy karimeen, the tanginess of fresh coconut-based curries, and the sweetness of tender coconut water, all while taking in the majestic views of the surrounding landscape.
With every bite and every turn of the boat,
discover the authentic essence of Kerala's backwaters and cuisine, in perfect harmony with the serene beauty of Amritapuri.

LIVE SEAFOOD COOKING

Experience the essence of seafood at SpiceRoutes, where freshness meets flavor.
Located near the sea and backwaters, our restaurant sources the freshest catches directly from local fishermen. Expert chefs craft dishes like karimeen pollichathu, crab thoran, and fish fry,
blending traditional Kerala spices with modern flavors. Savor the succulent flavors of the ocean, expertly balanced to delight your senses.
With a focus on Kerala cuisine, SpiceRoutes offers a culinary journey through the coastal regions of India, showcasing the rich seafood heritage of the state.

UNIQUENESS IN EVERY BITE

At SpiceRoutes, we proudly showcase the rich culinary heritage of Kerala by incorporating
local recipes into our innovative dishes. Under the guidance of world-class chefs, our culinary team expertly blends traditional flavors with modern twists,
creating a unique gastronomic experience. By using locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques, we craft authentic Kerala dishes
with a contemporary flair, transporting your taste buds on a journey through the spice routes of India.
Every bite is a testament to our passion for preserving local flavors.

BEACHSIDE BALCONIES

Perched above the tranquil shores, SpiceRoutes' beachside balconies offer a serene ambiance,
perfect for a romantic dinner or a relaxing evening with friends. As the sun dips into the horizon, casting a warm golden glow, indulge in our delectable cuisine,
expertly crafted to delight your senses. Savor the freshest seafood, authentic Kerala dishes, and international flavors,
all while taking in the soothing sounds of the waves and the breathtaking views of the ocean.
A truly unforgettable dining experience awaits.

BEACHSIDE RESTO BAR

Unwind at SpiceRoutes' beachside restobar, where the rhythms of the ocean blend with the beats of lively music.
Perched on the shore, our restobar offers an idyllic setting to relax and rejuvenate. Savor expertly crafted cocktails,
refreshing beers, and delectable snacks, all while taking in the breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea. As the sun sets, the restobar transforms into a vibrant hub,
perfect for socializing, dancing, or simply enjoying a tranquil evening by the sea.
